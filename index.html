Projects & Publications

Stealthy Cross-Modal Prompt Injection Attack

Ongoing | Vizuara AI

Identifying critical blind spots in Large Vision-Language Models (LVLMs) safety. We propose novel cross-modal prompt injection techniques to bypass safety filters, highlighting vulnerabilities in current systems.

Scientific-Text-to-ELI5-GIFs

Ongoing | Independent Research

Inventing a novel way to learn science through "Explain Like I'm 5" (ELI5) cartoon videos representing everyday analogies. Building a novel, high-quality dataset containing intuition-based, ELI5 analogies for complex scientific texts to aid in public understanding and model training.

Universal Evaluation Metric for Games-Based Learning with Cards

Dec 2024 | Independent Research

Designed a novel metric for quantifying the educational value obtained by students through playing educational card games, aiming to standardize assessment in game-based learning.
